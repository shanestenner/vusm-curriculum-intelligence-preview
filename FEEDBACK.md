# Feedback — VUSM Curriculum Intelligence Pilot

After you've used the skill for a session or two (or whenever you have a moment), please share your experience. The feedback shapes what stays, what changes, and whether to expand the pilot.

## REDCap survey

**Survey link:** _<Shane will paste the REDCap survey URL here once the form is published.>_

The survey takes ~5 minutes. It's anonymous unless you choose to include your name. You can submit it more than once if you'd like to share separate observations.

## What we're hoping to learn

The survey covers three areas:

1. **Setup experience.** Was it easy to install? Any friction in the upload flow?
2. **Quality of Claude's answers.** Were they accurate, useful, fast? Did Claude get the VUSM curriculum content *right*, or did it hallucinate things that aren't true about our program?
3. **What would make it more valuable.** What's missing? What would you ask if you trusted it more?

The most useful single piece of feedback for us: **specific examples of things Claude got wrong** about VUSM courses, MEPOs, sessions, or coverage. We can fix those.

## What to do if Claude says something wrong

The survey has a "safety/accuracy concern" question — please use it if Claude says something incorrect about a clinical fact, a VUSM course, a MEPO, a session, or a faculty member's teaching. Include the question you asked and a paraphrase of Claude's response.

For anything truly urgent (e.g., Claude is giving advice that could affect patient care decisions), email Shane directly rather than waiting for the survey.

## How to opt out of the pilot

Either:
- Reply to Shane's onboarding email asking to wind down, **or**
- Email Shane the line *"please revoke my Curriculum Intelligence key"*.

Either way, Shane will revoke your API key (≤60 seconds) and remove you from the pilot roster. The skill in your Claude app stops working immediately after revocation; you can delete it from Settings → Skills at your convenience.
